The merit of vintage watches:

Elegant designs due to the goal of maximizing functionality and reducing size.

This leads to smaller watches that fit much nicer on the wrists and slide under the cuff.

Many of the watch companies of the past are no longer here today do to the quartz crisis.

That being said there is value on these watches as they are sought after by the watch enthusiasts

A good vintage watch is a novelty item. Its not for everyone and may not be up to todays standards in terms of technology and condition.

The merit of quartz watches:

impeccable quartz accuracy.

Once set you dont have to worry about winding of mainspring like in a manual-wind.

No adjustments needed; there is no main spring and time will not slow down or speed up depending on how much reserve there is.

Quartz movements tend to be more robust as there arent as much moving parts.
