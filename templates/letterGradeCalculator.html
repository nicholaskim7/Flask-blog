Letter grade calculator GUI

GUI from Tkinter library

Used to calculate my final letter grade when I took Algorithms and Data Structures

Why I Made It?

I think it is always frustrating not knowing where you stand academically, especially when all the grades are in.

It may take longer than expected to find out your earned letter grade and in order to alleviate this uncertainty, I made a very simple user interface that I can take with me to future classes so that I will no longer be stumped.

The only thing to consider is the actual letter grades in use and cutoffs for the specific professor, and I can calculate any letter grade.
